Neurodivergent Coaching

Price Â£50 Time 50 Minutes

This support is aimed at students studying at University, but works for A Level students as well. The support has two elements, a course lasting between 10 and 16 weeks exploring ways to manage the impact a neurodivergent brain has on studying, the other being ongoing study support sessions. The sessions can be extended to support any ongoing need for study support. This can work well alongside the wellbeing coaching. These sessions can be offered online or in the community at present, for more information and to explore your personal needs please message for a free 15 minute consultation.

Why Neurodivergent Coaching

1

Improving Organizational Skills and Time Management

A Neurodivergent coach can help students develop personalised strategies and use tools to improve their organisational skills, create effective schedules, and prioritise tasks, leading to better academic performance and reduced stress.

2

Enhancing Focus and Reducing Procrastination

To identify specific distractions and develop techniques to improve focus and concentration. This might include creating structured study routines, breaking tasks into manageable chunks, and using reminders and incentives to stay on track.

3

Building Self-Confidence and Coping Strategies

Build self-confidence by setting realistic goals, celebrating small achievements, and developing coping strategies for handling setbacks. This support can empower students to overcome obstacles, stay motivated, and believe in their abilities to succeed.
