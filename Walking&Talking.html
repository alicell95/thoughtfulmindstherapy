Reasons for Walking & Talking Therapy

1

Enhanced Relaxation & Reduced Anxiety

Engaging in physical activity like walking can naturally reduce anxiety and promote relaxation. The act of walking helps release endorphins, which can improve mood and reduce stress levels.

2

Improved Mental & Physical Health

Walking is a low-impact exercise that can benefit both mental and physical health. If you find it difficult to sit still for traditional therapy sessions, walking provides an alternative that keeps the body active. Being connected to nature and grounding yourself helps to increase mindfulness and slows us do.

3

Enhanced Creativity & Problem-Solving

The movement and change of scenery involved in walking can stimulate creative thinking and improve problem-solving skills. You can feel more comfortable in nature, making it easier to open up and say what you need to say.
